Capital Markets Revenue Prediction
Highlighted Skills:
Summary:
Built a time series forecasting model to predict Capital Markets monthly income. Cross-validated between multiple models (SARIMA, TBATS, XGBoost, LightGBM). Settled on a hybrid tree-based model that I featured engineered in economic trend and lagged delta and moving average data. Used Hyperopt Bayesian hyper-parameter optimization with a custom expanding window objective function to minimize loss. Improved the previous model's average prediction error from 24.79% to 3.96%
